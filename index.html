TEXTILE PRINT DESIGN

Hello. Thank you for visiting my site.

 

My name is Natalie Babak. I'm a Fashion Illustrator and Textile Print Designer. I enjoy creating beautiful and detailed artwork, emotive illustrations and eye catching prints. Many of my illustrations are digital, however traditional painting is my passion and always an option.

 

I spent many years as an apparel and accessories designer, creating concepts to shaping the final product. I collaborate with fashion  designers and studios, brands and magazines providing the following services:

 

    • Fashion Sketches and Illustrations
    • Beauty and Lifestyle Illustrations
    • Textile Design
    • Surface Print Design
    • Technical Flats and Tech Packs

 

Work can be sent around the world, by mail or online.
